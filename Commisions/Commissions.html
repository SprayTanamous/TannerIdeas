Commissions

Interested in commissioning a custom sword, dagger, sheath, etc.? Please contact me at support@tannerideas.com. In your email, attach reference photos and add as many clarifying details as possible. They help me gauge the difficulty of the build. All custom orders ship with virtual instructions and all the LEGO® pieces necessary to build the model. Serious buyers only.

Current custom build rates (subject to change):

  • General price estimates for custom orders with fewer than 1000 bricks are $150-$300 plus a $75 design fee
  • For large, custom orders with over 1000 bricks (like the Yoru sword) the design fee increases to minimum of $150. I’ll need some additional time to create a model and provide you with a better cost estimate
  • If your build requires rare or especially expensive bricks, the price could increase significantly based on current market rates

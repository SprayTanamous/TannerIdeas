About

My name is Tanner, and I am currently a college student. I love building LEGO® in my free time and had the idea of selling my creations. I aim to create and build fantasy-inspired swords, daggers, sheaths, etc., using 100% real LEGO® bricks, and of course, no adhesives. Over the past few years, I have worked toward my goal, building over 40 unique builds.

Interessted in commissioning a custom sword, dagger, sheath, etc.? Please get in touch with me at support@tannerideas.com. More information can be found here: Custom Swords.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact me at support@tannerideas.com, or navigate to the FAQ page. I will try my best to get back to you in a timely manner.
